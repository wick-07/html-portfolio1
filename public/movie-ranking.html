Best Movies According To Akash

My top 3 movies of all-time

The Batman Trilogy

Batman, also known as Bruce Wayne, is a legendary DC Comics superhero created by Bob Kane and Bill Finger in 1939. Unlike most superheroes, he has no superpowers but relies on intellect, martial arts, detective skills, and advanced technology. After witnessing his parents' murder as a child, he dedicates his life to fighting crime in Gotham City as the Dark Knight. The Dark Knight Trilogy is widely regarded as one of the greatest superhero film series due to its deep storytelling, complex characters, and realistic approach to the Batman mythos. Christopher Nolan’s vision grounded the superhero genre in reality, focusing on themes of fear, chaos, and redemption.

The Others

The Others (2001), directed by Alejandro Amenábar, is widely praised for its masterful storytelling, eerie atmosphere, and psychological depth. The film, starring Nicole Kidman, is a gothic horror thriller that relies on suspense rather than jump scares, creating an unsettling experience.

The soul

the animated film by Pixar and Disney. Directed by Pete Docter, Soul follows Joe Gardner, a middle school music teacher and aspiring jazz musician who unexpectedly finds himself in the afterlife. As he navigates the realms of the Great Before and Great Beyond, he learns profound lessons about purpose, passion, and the meaning of life.
